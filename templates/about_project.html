Music Recommendation System

Authentication

Users can authenticate with Spotify to access their account information and preferences. Once authenticated, they can seamlessly integrate their Spotify account with our recommendation system.

Search and Identification

Users can search for music by providing the title and artist name or by recording a sample for identification using Shazam. Our system employs advanced algorithms to accurately identify songs and provide relevant recommendations based on the user's search queries.

Personalized Recommendations

Our recommendation system utilizes machine learning techniques to analyze user preferences, listening history, and behavior patterns to generate personalized music recommendations. By understanding each user's unique tastes and preferences, we ensure that the recommendations are tailored to their individual preferences.

Social Integration

Users can connect with friends and share their favorite music recommendations through social integration features. They can follow their friends, view their listening activity, and discover new music based on their recommendations. Social interactions enhance the user experience and foster a sense of community among music enthusiasts.

Playlist Generation

Our system enables users to create custom playlists based on their preferences and mood. Users can easily add recommended tracks to their playlists or explore curated playlists tailored to specific genres, moods, or occasions. Playlist generation features make it convenient for users to discover and organize their favorite music in one place.

Music Discovery

In addition to personalized recommendations, our system facilitates music discovery by introducing users to new artists, genres, and tracks that align with their interests. Through curated playlists, artist recommendations, and exploration tools, users can expand their musical horizons and discover hidden gems within the vast landscape of music.
